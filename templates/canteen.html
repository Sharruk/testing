About SSN Canteen

SSN Canteen is the heart of campus dining, serving delicious and hygienic food to students, faculty, and staff. We pride ourselves on offering a variety of South Indian and North Indian dishes, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices. Our dedicated team ensures fresh preparation and maintains high standards of cleanliness and quality.
